Who am I?

I have always had the need to delve into the different means of communication and expression that have been crossing my path; drawing, writing, photography, sociology and psychology ... So I studied audiovisual comunication and sociology degree for a way where try to create, express and transmit. Recently, halfway between marketing, with growth hacking and graphic design I stumbled upon user experience and decided to go for it, so I studied a specialization course in Ux design and then that's where I am: Thinking that product creation could be the cornerstone of the disciplines in which I previously immersed myself.
